Phase 5 — the hex: one number, slow, build-revealing

Was: a 0–100 score running alongside permanent XP (two ranks) + a display trick that capped at 92 and never cleanly maxed. Now: one number per axis, derived from XP.

Slow & maxable

Tiny slivers (3–6%) at week 1; a maxed axis ≈ 3 yr balanced heavy training. Real top cliff, clean 100%.

Catch-up

Neglected axes earn up to ×2; over-fed axes slow near cap. Power can't pin; weakness-work pays off.

Build-revealing

The hex shows your real shape — balanced training is the efficient route to a full hex.
