## `cal.pushup`

Subject: Push-Up form breakdown. Apparatus: floor only, side view.

Panel 1 label: "PLANK SET". Athlete in high plank, hands under lower chest, straight line from head to heels, ribs tucked, glutes tight.
Panel 2 label: "DESCEND". Athlete lowering as one rigid plank, elbows tracking diagonally back about 30-45 degrees, chest and hips moving together.
Panel 3 label: "DEPTH". Athlete at bottom with chest close to floor, neck neutral, elbows close to body, no sagging hips.
Panel 4 label: "LOCKOUT". Athlete pressing back to straight elbows and active shoulders, same plank line as start.

## `cal.5-dips`

Panel 2 label: "LOWER". Athlete descending under control, elbows tracking back, torso slightly forward, shoulders not shrugged.
Panel 3 label: "BOTTOM". Athlete at deepest controlled bottom, shoulders near or slightly below elbows, chest proud, no collapse.
Panel 4 label: "PRESS OUT". Athlete pressing to full support lockout, bars pushed down, shoulders active.
