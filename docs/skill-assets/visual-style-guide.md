# Skill Asset Visual Style Guide

Date: 2026-05-17

## Canonical Movement Avatar

Use `docs/skill-assets/unbound-movement-avatar-reference.png` as the character reference for future generated skill assets.

Core traits:
- Webtoon/anime fitness avatar, not photorealistic.
- Black messy hair, black sleeveless training top, black joggers, black wrist wraps, black trainers.
- Athletic but not bodybuilder bulky.
- Dark charcoal app background with teal/cyan rim accents and small amber motion highlights.
- Keep the same outfit, hair, proportions, and silhouette across every skill.

## Prompt Anchor

Use this prompt fragment in future asset prompts:

> Match the canonical Unbound movement avatar: black messy anime hair, black sleeveless training top, black joggers, black wrist wraps, black trainers, athletic webtoon/anime proportions, dark charcoal app background, teal/cyan rim lighting, small amber motion accents. Keep the same character identity and outfit across panels.

## Instructional Panel Rules

- The visual should teach the form step by itself.
- Use one step per panel.
- Declare the camera angle in every prompt: side view, front view, back view, or 3/4 view.
- Keep the same camera angle across adjacent panels unless a different angle teaches a specific detail better.
- Make equipment physically coherent. Hands must wrap around the bar/rings/handles in the same perspective as the equipment, with believable wrist alignment.
- For swing or path-based movements, prefer clean side view with a fixed horizontal bar so the body path is readable.
- Motion arrows should show the actual path of the body or limb.
- Keep labels short: step number plus movement word.
- Avoid decorative or cinematic poses that do not explain the skill.
- Avoid card-inside-card framing.
- Avoid extra paragraphs inside the image.
