## `cal.pseudo-planche-pushup`

Subject: Pseudo-Planche Push-Up form breakdown. Apparatus: floor only, side view.

Panel 1 label: "LEAN SET". Athlete in push-up plank with hands low near hips/lower ribs, shoulders clearly forward past wrists, ribs down, glutes tight.
Panel 2 label: "PROTRACT". Athlete pushing floor away with rounded upper back/scapular protraction, elbows straight, hollow body line.
Panel 3 label: "HOLD LEAN". Athlete descending while shoulders remain forward, elbows tracking back, chest and hips moving together, no shift into normal push-up.
Panel 4 label: "SAME SHAPE". Athlete pressing back to the same forward lean and protracted shoulder position, body still one rigid line.

## `pl.straddle-planche`
