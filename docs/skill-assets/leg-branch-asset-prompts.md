## Foundation

## `ld.step-up`

Subject: Step-Up form breakdown. Apparatus: knee-height box, side view.

Panel 1 label: "PLANT". Athlete facing box with whole lead foot planted on top, torso tall, trail foot on floor.
Panel 2 label: "DRIVE". Athlete driving through lead heel/midfoot, front knee tracking over toes, trail leg quiet and not pushing hard.
Panel 3 label: "STAND". Athlete fully tall on top of box, hip and knee extended, balance controlled.
Panel 4 label: "CONTROL". Athlete stepping down slowly from the box, knee aligned over toes, soft controlled descent.

## `ld.deep-squat`

Subject: Deep Squat form breakdown. Apparatus: floor only, three-quarter side view.
Panel 1 label: "STANCE". Athlete standing with feet shoulder width, slight toe turn-out, tripod foot pressure shown.
Panel 2 label: "SINK". Athlete descending between hips, knees tracking toes, heels flat.
Panel 3 label: "BREATHE". Athlete in full deep squat, hips below knees, chest open, calm active hold.
Panel 4 label: "RESET". Athlete standing back up with control before the squat shape collapses.

## `ld.glute-bridge`

Subject: Glute Bridge form breakdown. Apparatus: floor mat, side view.
Panel 1 label: "RIBS DOWN". Athlete supine, heels under knees, ribs tucked, arms relaxed on floor.
Panel 2 label: "DRIVE". Athlete lifting hips by driving through heels, glutes initiating the motion.
Panel 3 label: "PAUSE". Athlete at full hip extension, straight shoulder-to-knee line, glutes squeezed, no low-back arch.
Panel 4 label: "LOWER". Athlete lowering with control, pelvis level, heels still planted.

## `ld.single-leg-glute-bridge`

Subject: Single-Leg Glute Bridge form breakdown. Apparatus: floor mat, side view.
Panel 1 label: "SET LEVEL". Athlete supine with one heel under knee, opposite leg extended, pelvis level.
Panel 2 label: "DRIVE". Athlete lifting hips from the working glute, free leg still extended and quiet.
Panel 3 label: "PAUSE". Athlete at top with hips level, ribs down, straight shoulder-to-knee line on working side.
Panel 4 label: "LOWER". Athlete lowering slowly without pelvis twisting or free leg swinging.

## `ld.shrimp-squat`

Subject: Shrimp Squat form breakdown. Apparatus: floor or thin knee pad, side view.
Panel 1 label: "ANKLE GRIP". Athlete balancing on one leg, opposite hand holding rear ankle, free arm forward.
Panel 2 label: "SLOW LOWER". Athlete descending under control, working heel planted, torso upright.
Panel 3 label: "SOFT TOUCH". Rear knee lightly touches floor or pad, no crash, hips square.
Panel 4 label: "STAND CLEAN". Athlete stands through working leg, rear leg not pushing.
